### FULL STACK DEVELOPER

As a passionate professional in the software engineering space, I thrive on the challenge of building
quality into maintainable, scalable, performant software systems that provide solutions to business
problems and produce 10x value. <br />
I also enjoy contributing to the overall process of reaching this end goal by building high-performing,
cross-functional, collaborative, independent, and responsible teams. Whether it is the establishment
of processes, practices, and principles via training and mentoring teams or the direct contribution of
architectural design/diagrams and writing clean maintainable code, I have a burning passion for
building software. <br />
Outside of work, I enjoy staying current with technology, cycling, and maintaining a healthy work-life
balance.
